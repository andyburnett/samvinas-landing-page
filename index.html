About Us

We are dedicated to supporting academics and research development teams, who need to bring deliberate creativity into their research processes.

Our products and services have been developed on the basis of more than 20 years experience in designing and running Ideas Labs, and other interdisciplinary collaboration workshops.

We have established Samvinas to bring this expertise to a wider audience.
